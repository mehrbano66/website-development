🍝Italian cuisine

>
  • Simple, comforting, and super popular worldwide.
  • It uses fresh ingredients like tomatoes, cheese, and olive oil.
  • Famousdishes include pizza, pasta, and lasagna.
  • Very diverse with a balance of flavors—sweet, sour, spicy, and savory.
  • Popular for noodles, fried rice, and stir-fry dishes.
  • It’s usually quick-cooked and flavorful.

    • 🌮Mexican cuisine

  • Fun and vibrant cuisine with bold flavors.

    🍣 Japanese cuisine

  • Light, clean, and focused on freshness.
  • Known for sushi, ramen, and seafood dishes.
  • It’s less oily and often considered a healthier option.
