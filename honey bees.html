Honey Bees.

  1. Honey bees make honey from flower nectar and store it in their hive as food.🍯
  2. They live together in a colony with thousands of bees.
  3. The queen bee lays up to about 2,000 eggs in one day.👑
  4. Honey bees help plants grow by pollinating flowers, which helps make fruits and vegetables.🌸
