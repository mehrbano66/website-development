How to make a Pizza?👩‍🍳

Make the dough

  1. In a bowl mix warm water, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  3. Add flour, salt, and oil.
  4. Knead into a soft dough.
  5. Cover and let it rest for 30-60 minutes until it rises.

    6. Shape the pizza

  6. Roll the dough into a round pizza base.
  7. Place it on a baking tray or pan.

    8. Add toppings

  8. Spread pizza sauce on the base.
  9. Add cheese.
  10. Add your favorite toppings.

    11. Bake

  11. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 12-15 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden.

    12. Serve

  12. Slice and enjoy your pizza!😋
