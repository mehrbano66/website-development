Great Wall of China

  1. It is one of the longest walls in the world.
  2. It was built over 2,000 years ago.
  3. The wall was made to protect China from enemies.
  4. Parts of the wall were built during the rule of Qin Shi Huang
  5. Soldiers used watchtowers on the wall to look for enemies.
  6. Today it is a very famous tourist place.
